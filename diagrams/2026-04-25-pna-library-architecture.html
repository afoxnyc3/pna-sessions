How Skills Land on an Agent

The library skill orchestrates the full install and sync cycle from library.yaml declaration to active symlink.

Declare in library.yaml

→
Step 2
Push to pna-library

→
Step 3

→
Step 4
Symlink resolves

Step 6
Librarian optimizes
